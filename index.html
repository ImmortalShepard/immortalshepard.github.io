About me

Being a game depeloper, I like playing games as well. Some of my favorite games include The Legend of Zelda series and the Mass Effect series. A game I spent a good amount of time with was Arena: Cyber Evolution (ACE), a 3v3 top down sports game. The community was rather small when my friends and I started playing and I decided to help out by streaming the tournaments for the EU server. I really enjoyed playing the game and being part of the community. Sadly the game had it's fair share of problems and never had a very big player base. Development was eventually stopped in 2018. ACE was definetly part of the initial reason why I wanted to be a game deveoper.
